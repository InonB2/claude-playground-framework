=== JOB DESCRIPTION ===

We are looking for a Technical Project Manager to join the Smart Sensing Group in C4I and Cyber. You will drive mission-critical, multidisciplinary projects that combine complex software and advanced algorithms, translating operational needs into real impact in a dynamic defense environment. If you enjoy end-to-end ownership, system thinking and working closely with cross-functional teams — join us.

=== RESPONSIBILITIES ===

- Own all system engineering activities across the project lifecycle
- Analyze customer needs and translate them into clear engineering requirements
- Work with development teams to design complex system architecture
- Prepare and participate in requirements and design reviews with stakeholders
- Serve as Product Owner in agile teams responsible for developing and testing software products
- Lead integration and testing of software and hardware, including hands-on technical work
- Deploy solutions at customer sites

=== REQUIREMENTS ===

- BSc in Software Engineering or Computer Science
- At least 3 years of experience in software development
- Experience in software project management or software system engineering
- Experience with Agile methodology - advantage
- High proficiency in English and Hebrew, spoken and written
- Willingness to travel abroad
- Strong leadership in a matrix environment, excellent communication skills and system thinking
- Only relevant applications will be answered
