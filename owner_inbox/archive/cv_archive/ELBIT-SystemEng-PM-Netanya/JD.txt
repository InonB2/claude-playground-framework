- Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, Computer Science, or Information Systems
- At least 3 years of experience in System Engineering and Product Management
- Knowledge and hands-on experience with integrated, large-scale systems
- A strong background and experience in artillery systems, algorithms and operational doctrine
- Strong teamwork skills and ability to lead cross-organizational processes
- Broad system-level thinking and capacity to plan and manage complex technological workflows
- Familiarity with software technologies and operational environments
- Strong technical problem-solving capabilities
- Creativity, high self-learning ability, strong analytical thinking
