## Key Decisions Made

- **SQLite over flat files** for session logging — enables queries, indexing, and future Supabase migration.
- **Telegram over Twilio/WhatsApp** as messaging bridge — free, official API, no business verification. Owner later found `npx claude-code-whatsapp` as a potential WhatsApp option to evaluate.
- **LYRA as mandatory pre-execution skill** — all complex/ambiguous prompts get L/Y/R/A treatment before execution.
- **Scratchpad-first rule** — no agent writes directly to production paths; all drafts go to `/scratchpad/` first.
- **14 agents** selected and inspired by myicor.com/team framework; improved for Inon's CPO/product context.
- **One CV per application** — each version tailored to the JD, archived with full metadata.
